Joysino Humanitarian Initiative

  • About
  • Mission
  • Vision
Joysino Humanitarian Initiative is fully committed in grass root community services through its well-structured humanitarian and social development programmes. These programmes have provided different forms of relieve to over 10,000 displaced people, scholarship opportunities to many local indigents and skills acquisition programs for the less privilege youths in local communities across Nigeria.
To give hope to the most vulnerable persons in our local communities and raise and nurture the next generation of excellent leaders, intentionally building our local communities free of drug abuse, cultism and banditry through our awareness, sensitization and rural outreach programs and providing relive supports to the less privilege through our humanitarian response schemes.
Our vision is to create thriving, drug-free, and united communities, cultivating a generation of compassionate leaders. We aim to provide hope, dignity, and opportunity through awareness, outreach, and humanitarian support, making Joysino Humanitarian Initiative a symbol of positive change and transformation.
