Joysino Humanitarian Initiative

  • About
  • Mission
  • Vision
Joysino Humanitarian Initiative is a non-governmental organization (NGO) founded to lead a social change in our communities across Nigeria. We are focused on raising young leaders of marginalized communities through scholarship programs, empowerment schemes, leadership and coaching programs. Our aim is to make our communities safer with access to basic amenities and to build a tribe of young generations free of drug abuse, cultism, banditry, and all manners of illegal activities.
To give hope to the most vulnerable persons in our local communities and raise and nurture the next generation of excellent leaders, intentionally building our local communities free of drug abuse, cultism and banditry through our awareness, sensitization and rural outreach programs and providing relive supports to the less privilege through our humanitarian response schemes.
To bring together many vioces that would help us make smarter chioces, so our youths can build healthy, safe and successful lives free of substance abuse, cultism and banditry through youth counseling services, gospel music, concepts on the streets/campuses, healthy education and intimacy talks. We also reach out to the less-privildge, orphanage homes, internally displays persons (IDPs).
